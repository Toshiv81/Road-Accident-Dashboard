# Road-Accident-Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes road accident data and presents key insights using a Power BI dashboard. The goal is to identify patterns, risk factors, and trends to improve road safety awareness and decision-making.

📊 Dataset Information
Total Records: 5,000+ accidents
Data includes:
Vehicle Type
Weather Conditions
Road Type
Light Conditions
Date of Accident
📈 Key Insights
🚗 Vehicle Type Analysis
Cars account for 77.8% of total accidents
Vans and goods vehicles contribute 5.5%
Motorcycles contribute ~4%

👉 Cars are the most accident-prone vehicle category.

🌦️ Weather Conditions
86.4% of accidents occur in fine weather
Only 10.2% occur during rain

👉 Good weather leads to higher accident rates due to overconfidence and higher traffic volume.

🛣️ Road Type
84.3% accidents occur on single carriageways
Dual carriageways account for 9.9%

👉 Single carriageways are high-risk areas.

🌙 Light Conditions
71.7% accidents occur during daylight
27.9% occur in darkness (with lights)

👉 Traffic density during the day contributes significantly to accidents.

Visualizations:

Bar charts

🎯 Objectives


Identify high-risk conditions

Understand accident distribution

Support data-driven road safety decisions

📌 Conclusion
The analysis reveals that:


Most accidents happen in daylight and good weather

Cars and single carriageways are major contributors
Road safety awareness should target daily driving behavior
